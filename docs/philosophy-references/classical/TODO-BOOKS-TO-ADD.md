## Priority 1: Purchase (worth it for KG extraction)
- **The Well-Trained Mind** (Susan Wise Bauer & Jessie Wise, 4th ed.) — ~$40
  https://wwnorton.com/books/9781324073734
  The single most important practical guide. Has stage-by-stage activity/materials lists for all subjects K–12.

## Priority 2: Archive.org free borrow (need logged-in browser session — can't automate)
Log in at archive.org, then borrow and save each:
- **Recovering the Lost Tools of Learning** (Douglas Wilson)
  https://archive.org/details/recoveringlostto00wils
  Practical school model, memorization/recitation activities, Grammar through Rhetoric structure.

- **How to Read a Book** (Mortimer Adler, 1972 revised ed.)
  https://archive.org/details/howtoreadabook1972edition
  Defines reading skill levels that map perfectly to Trivium stages.

- **The Paideia Proposal** (Mortimer Adler)
  https://archive.org/details/paideiaproposal00mort
  Three pedagogical modes (didactic instruction, coached practice, Socratic seminar).

- **Climbing Parnassus** (Tracy Lee Simmons)
  https://archive.org/details/climbingparnassu0000simm
  Latin/Greek instruction principles and rationale.
