## Detailed Notes

### Confirmed Programs with Known Commission Rates

1. **All About Learning Press** -- 15% commission, 180-day cookie, free to join. Includes banners, training materials, and product review opportunities. One of the most generous in the homeschool space.

2. **Sonlight** -- 6% commission, 90-day first-click cookie, $50 payout threshold, monthly PayPal payouts.

3. **BookShark** -- 6% commission, 90-day first-click cookie, $50 payout threshold, monthly PayPal payouts. (BookShark is a secular spinoff of Sonlight with identical affiliate terms.)

4. **Alpha Omega Publications (Horizons Math)** -- 4% commission, 10-day cookie. Managed through Awin network. Covers all AOP products including Horizons, Monarch, LIFEPAC, and Switched-On Schoolhouse.

5. **Pandia Press** -- 10% commission, 30-day cookie. PayPal payouts quarterly. Covers Real Science Odyssey and History Quest.

6. **Apologia Science** -- 10% commission on qualifying purchases (excludes lab equipment and live classes). Also provides a 10% off promo code for your audience. Monthly PayPal payouts on the 15th via Post Affiliate Pro.

7. **Bright Ideas Press (Mystery of History)** -- 30% per sale. One of the highest commission rates in the homeschool curriculum space.

8. **Beautiful Feet Books** -- 10% commission, 45-day cookie, bi-annual PayPal payouts (June and December). Bonus tiers: $25 for 5-9 conversions/month, $60 for 10-14, $105 for 15+. Managed via Tapfiliate.

9. **Notgrass History** -- 12% per sale. Application subject to review; they look for alignment with their mission.

10. **Simply Charlotte Mason** -- Tiered: 5% base, 10% after $5,000 in annual sales. 60-day cookie. Free to join.

11. **Moving Beyond the Page** -- 5% on all purchases by referred customers for one full year from referral date.

12. **Calvert Homeschool** -- 4% commission, 30-day cookie. Managed through Awin network.

13. **Master Books** -- 8-10% on first qualified sale, 2% on subsequent sales (up to 5 orders total per referred customer). 60-day cookie, $50 minimum payout. Invitation-only program.

14. **Daily Wonder** -- 25% cash back on purchases made with your code. Affiliates also provide a 10% off coupon to their community.

### Confirmed Programs with Unknown Commission Rates

15. **Logic of English** -- Has an affiliate program through iDevAffiliate. 30-day cookie. Commission rate disclosed upon application.

16. **Memoria Press** -- Has an affiliate program at id.memoriapress.com. Commission details not publicly listed.

17. **IEW (Institute for Excellence in Writing)** -- Has an affiliate program at iew.com/affiliates. Commission details provided after application.

18. **RightStart Mathematics** -- Has an affiliate program through iDevAffiliate. Monthly commission payouts. Must post about RightStart at least once per month.

19. **Well-Trained Mind Press** -- Support article titled "Does Well-Trained Mind Press have an affiliate program?" exists on their help center, suggesting one exists. Contact support@welltrainedmind.com for details.

20. **Earth Schooling** -- Has an affiliate area at earthschooling.info. Details behind login; contact them directly.

### Referral/Influencer Programs (Not Traditional Affiliate)

21. **Brave Writer** -- Refer-a-Friend program only. Both referrer and new customer get $10 store credit. Not a cash commission. Uses Refersion platform (bravewriter.refersion.com).

22. **Timberdoodle** -- Influencer/blog review team rather than an affiliate program. Compensation is in products, not cash. Must have established blog or YouTube channel to apply.

## Methodology

Each company was searched using web search queries targeting "[Company Name] affiliate program sign up commission." Results were cross-referenced across affiliate directories (GetLasso, Affiliate Program DB, Authority Hacker), company websites, and homeschool blogger disclosures. Research conducted March 15, 2026.
