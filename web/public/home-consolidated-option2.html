What changed: tabs + inline search/filter inside the Lessons card; nav flattened; /lessons redirects here; everything else is identical. Paid users get the same structure plus a calendar and children cards below.
Why this works for both tiers:
  • Compass (free) tier: 1–3 lessons is a small list — tabs and search are quiet but present, no clutter.
  • Homestead+: 20–50 lessons — the tabs / search / filters earn their keep. The calendar and per-child stats slot in below the lessons card (not mocked; same as today).
  • Redirect plan: old /lessons → 301 to /home. Old /lessons?favorite=true → /home?tab=favorites. Query params preserve deep links.
  • One-time cost: move the lesson-list component out of /lessons/page.tsx into a <LessonLibrary> component that renders inside both the Home page and any future surfaces. Maybe half a day of work.
